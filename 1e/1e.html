ROLLS ROYCE

This legendary brand finds its roots in Henry Royce's electrical and mechanical business, which started in 1884 and went on to become a car manufacturing company in 1906. Royce made his first car in 1904, and was soon introduced to Charles Rolls, a proprietor of a car dealership.
