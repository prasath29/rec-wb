The Mini Hatch (stylised as MINI hatch, or MINI Hardtop in the US), also known as Mini Cooper, Mini One, or simply the Mini, is a three-door or five door hatchback first introduced in early 2000 by German luxury automotive company BMW under the Mini marque.

The second generation was launched in 2006 and the third in 2014.

A convertible version was introduced in 2004, with the second generation following in 2008.
